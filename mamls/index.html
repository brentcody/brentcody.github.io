About
MAMLS at VCU will be a gathering of set theorists working on large cardinals, inner models, forcing and relative consistency results, sponsored by the NSF and the Department of Mathematics and Applied Mathematics at Virginia Commonwealth University. The meeting begins with breakfast at 9:00 AM on April 1, talks starting at 9:30 AM that day, and the meeting ends at 11:45 AM on April 2. Thanks to J. Alex McWhorter for desiging the poster.
While graduate students, young researchers, female mathematicians and members of underrepresented groups are particularly encouraged to apply for travel support, it should be stressed that any participants without their own sources of funding are eligible to apply. Requests will be handled on a case-by-case basis within the limits of the budget. To apply for travel support, contact vcumamls@gmail.com. For other questions, please send an email to bmcody@vcu.edu.
